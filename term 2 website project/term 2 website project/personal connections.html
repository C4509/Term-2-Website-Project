My Personal Connections To StarCraft

I don't have any personal connections to StarCraft. However, there are many reasons that other players have given for loving the game. Here are some of those reasons.

  • Luck is balanced well with skill
  • Playing StarCraft pushes your mind into a new zone of consciousness, activating all aspects of your brain
  • Extremely well articulated in terms of unit and race interactions.
  • The community is incredibly kind and helpful
  • The game contains amazing depth and balance
  • Developing good strategies actually requires tons of practice
